  Sub-states that matter

  - Delegation: some/all cold accounts have an approved delegate
  - Frozen: accounts frozen by freeze authority
  - V1 vs V2 tree: cold accounts could span different tree versions
  - Multiple cold accounts: fragmented balance across many small accounts
  - Mint not yet decompressed: can only receive cold, no ATA possible

  Mint-level states

  - Compressed mint (not decompressed yet) -- only cold minting possible
  - Decompressed mint -- hot ATAs can be created
  - With/without freeze authority
  - With/without metadata for these
